Frequently Asked Questions

RFQ & Process Questions

What information do I need to submit an RFQ?

At minimum: your industry, the services you need, a project description, and your timeline. The more detail you provide — BOM complexity, compliance requirements, target volumes — the more accurate our initial assessment. If you are early in the process, a rough description is enough to start the conversation.

How quickly will I receive a quote?

Our engineering team reviews every submission within 24 hours and delivers a detailed quote within 3–5 business days. Urgent projects can be fast-tracked — select "Urgent" in the timeline field and we prioritize accordingly.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

No. GPW handles programs from pilot builds of 10–50 units through sustained production runs of 10,000+ units per month. We scale with your demand. During the scoping phase, we align production capacity to your volume forecast so pricing reflects actual requirements.

What does the quote include?

A GPW quote breaks down per-unit pricing by assembly stage, NRE costs, lead time to first article, production ramp timeline, recommended testing protocols, and any tooling or fixture requirements. You see exactly where cost and time go at every step.

Can I visit the facility before committing?

Yes. We encourage facility tours for prospective OEM partners. Monterrey is a 2-hour flight from Austin, Dallas, or Phoenix. Contact us to schedule a visit with our engineering and operations team.

Do you handle procurement and sourcing of components?

GPW can manage full kitting and procurement based on your approved vendor list (AVL), or we can work with customer-furnished materials (CFM). We determine the right approach during the scoping phase.

What industries do you serve?

GPW serves 8 industries: AI & Server Rack Assembly, Industrial Equipment, Telecommunications, Medical Devices, Automotive, Energy, Aerospace, and Appliances & White Goods. Each vertical has industry-specific assembly processes, traceability requirements, and quality standards.

Is my project information confidential?

Yes. All RFQ submissions are treated as confidential. GPW operates under strict NDAs with all customers. We never share project details, BOM data, or proprietary specifications with third parties. If you require a mutual NDA before submitting project details, contact us and we arrange it before you submit.
