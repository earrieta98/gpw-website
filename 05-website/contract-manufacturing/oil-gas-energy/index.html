The Reality of Oilfield

Downhole, you do not get a second chance.

Oil and gas parts do not operate in a lab. They operate a mile underground, submerged in seawater, or inside a pipeline carrying corrosive fluids at 5,000 PSI. When a part fails, you do not send it back for warranty — you mobilize a workover rig and hope the fishing tool can retrieve it. Material, traceability, and finish are not specs. They are insurance.

FAQ

Oil & Gas machining — answered.

Can you machine Inconel and other superalloys?

Yes. Inconel 718, 625, Hastelloy, and Monel. These materials require rigid machine setups, ceramic or carbide insert tooling, slow speeds, aggressive coolant, and experienced operators. Our partner shops have dedicated programs for exotic alloys.

Do you provide NACE-compliant materials?

Yes. We source materials per NACE MR0175 / ISO 15156 with full MTR documentation showing chemical composition, mechanical properties, heat treatment, and heat lot traceability. Compliance is verified at the sourcing stage — before machining begins.

How fast can you deliver rush parts?

Expedited service is available for downtime-critical situations. Contact us directly — phone or email — and we prioritize your job. We understand that a rig at $50K/day does not wait for standard lead times.

Can you machine API-threaded components?

Yes. API 5B threading — buttress, round, and line pipe threads — with gauge verification (go/no-go) on every part.

Do you handle large valve bodies and manifolds?

Yes. We machine large-envelope parts on our biggest mills and lathes. Contact us with your dimensions and material — we confirm capacity within 24 hours.

Can you coordinate pressure testing?

Yes. Through qualified partner facilities — hydrostatic and pneumatic testing per your specification. Test reports included in the documentation package.
