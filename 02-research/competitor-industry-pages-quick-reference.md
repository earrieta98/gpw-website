## Naming Conventions

### H1 Title Patterns

**Formula 1 (Most Common):** `[Industry Name] Manufacturing`
- Examples: "Medical Device Manufacturing", "Aerospace & Defense Manufacturing"
- Used by: PARPRO, SEACOMP, COASTLINE

**Formula 2 (Descriptive):** `[Industry Name] Solutions` or `[Industry Name] Services`
- Examples: "Computing & Storage Solutions"
- Used by: PARPRO (occasional)

- Examples: "PCB Assembly Services", "Box Build Services"
- Used by: Milwaukee

**Formula 4 (Market-Focused):** `[Market Name] Innovation`
- Examples: "Private Markets Innovation"

## Recommendations for GPW

### Best Practices Identified

1. **PARPRO's Model is Cleanest**
   - Dedicated /industries/ hub
   - Equal treatment of 6 verticals
   - Clear URL pattern
   - Searchable and SEO-friendly

2. **SEACOMP's Hybrid Works for Scale**
   - Company size + industry segmentation
   - Good for large EMS shops with diverse clients
   - Hub-based approach allows flexible content

3. **Avoid Milwaukee's Approach (for your use case)**
   - Service-focused pages are weak for vertical positioning
   - No industry SEO benefits
   - GPW needs to own "AI Server Manufacturing" keywords, not just "PCB Assembly"

4. **Naming Recommendation**
   - Use: `[Industry] Manufacturing Services` or `[Industry] Assembly`
   - Examples: "AI Server Rack Assembly", "Telecom Equipment Manufacturing"
   - Avoid: Generic "Solutions" or "Services" (competes less effectively)

### Content Recommendations per Industry Page

Based on competitor analysis, each industry page should include:

1. **H1:** `[Industry Name] [Service Type]`
2. **Industry Overview:** What this vertical requires (quality, certifications, timing)
3. **GPW's Capabilities:** Why GPW is suited (relevant case studies, certifications)
4. **Key Services:** Which of GPW's services matter most (cable harness, box build, testing)
5. **Certifications/Standards:** ISO, FDA, ITAR, RoHS, etc. relevant to that vertical
6. **CTA:** RFQ button, "Get a Quote", "Download Datasheet"
7. **Keywords:** Industry name + manufacturing + assembly + relevant specialty
