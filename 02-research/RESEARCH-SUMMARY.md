## Research Overview

This research documents how 6 major EMS competitors structure their industry-specific pages, with the goal of identifying best practices for GPW's site architecture.

### Competitors Analyzed
1. **SEACOMP** (seacomp.com) - 7 industries + 2 customer segments
2. **Coastline International** (coastlineintl.com) - 2 categories (Medical + General)
3. **PARPRO** (parpro.com) - 6 dedicated industries
4. **Milwaukee Electronics** (milwaukeeelectronics.com) - 0 industries (service-focused)
5. **Creation Technologies** (creation-technologies.com) - 4 markets (fintech-focused)
6. **Sparton DeLeon** (spartondeleon.com) - Access issues (data unavailable)

## Key Findings

### Industry Coverage Distribution

| Competitor | Industry Count | Approach | URL Pattern |
|---|---|---|---|
| SEACOMP | 7 | Hub + customer segments | `/who-we-help/` |
| PARPRO | 6 | Dedicated verticals | `/industries/[slug]/` |
| COASTLINE | 2 | Binary (Medical + General) | `/medical/`, `/electronics/` |
| MILWAUKEE | 0 | Service-focused (no industries) | `/manufacture/` |
| CREATION | 4 | Market-focused (fintech) | Homepage hub |

### Industries Most Frequently Featured

**Common (4-5 competitors):**
- Medical Device / Life Sciences (4 competitors)

**Moderate (2-3 competitors):**
- Industrial Equipment / Controls (2 competitors)
- Aerospace & Defense (2 competitors)
- Telecom / Communications (1 competitor, but PARPRO includes it)

**Rarely Covered (0-1 competitors):**
- AI / Data Center Servers: 0 competitors
- Automotive Sub-assemblies: 0 competitors
- Energy Sector Equipment: 0 competitors
- Appliances & White Goods: 0 competitors

### URL Structure Patterns Found

**Pattern 1: Dedicated URLs (PARPRO - Best for SEO)**
```
/industries/aerospace-defense/
/industries/medical/
/industries/communications/
```
*Advantage:* Clear, searchable, optimal for vertical SEO

**Pattern 2: Hub-Based (SEACOMP - Flexible)**
```
/who-we-help/ (with internal navigation to verticals)
```
*Advantage:* Allows multiple segmentation paths (industry + company size)

**Pattern 3: Grouped Categories (COASTLINE - Simple)**
```
/medical/
/electronics/
```
*Advantage:* Easy to manage, but groups dissimilar industries together

**Pattern 4: Service-Focused (MILWAUKEE - Avoidable)**
```
/manufacture/ (with tabs for PCB, Box Build, etc.)
```
*Disadvantage:* No vertical SEO, weak for industry decision-makers

### Page Naming Conventions

**Formula 1 (Most Common):** `[Industry] Manufacturing`
- Examples: "Medical Device Manufacturing", "Aerospace & Defense Manufacturing"
- Found on: PARPRO, SEACOMP, COASTLINE

**Formula 2 (Descriptive):** `[Industry] Services/Solutions`
- Examples: "Computing & Storage Solutions"
- Less common but effective

**Formula 3 (Avoid):** Service names as titles
- Examples: "PCB Assembly Services", "Box Build Services"
- Weak for vertical targeting (See Milwaukee's approach)

## Recommendations for GPW

### Architecture Recommendation: PARPRO Model + SEACOMP Content Depth

**Why PARPRO?**
- Cleanest structure for vertical targeting
- 6 dedicated industries (close to GPW's 8)
- SEO-friendly URL pattern
- Clear navigation for OEM buyers

**Why Add SEACOMP Elements?**
- Allows future secondary segmentation (company size, geography, etc.)
- Hub-based approach is more flexible as company grows
- Content depth of SEACOMP's industry pages is higher

### Proposed URL Structure

```
/industries/ (Hub page - all 8 industries listed)

/industries/ai-server-rack-assembly/
/industries/industrial-equipment-assembly/
/industries/telecom-hardware-assembly/
/industries/medical-device-assembly/
/industries/automotive-sub-assemblies/
/industries/energy-sector-equipment/
/industries/aerospace-sub-assemblies/
/industries/appliances-white-goods-assembly/
```

### Differentiation Opportunity

GPW should **own the verticals competitors ignore:**

| Vertical | Competitor Coverage | GPW Opportunity |
|---|---|---|
| AI Server Assembly | 0/6 competitors | **Untapped - High value** |
| Telecom Hardware | 1/6 competitors (PARPRO only) | **Underserved** |
| Automotive Sub-assemblies | 0/6 competitors | **Completely untapped** |
| Energy Sector Equipment | 0/6 competitors | **Completely untapped** |

This means GPW can own long-tail keywords that competitors don't target:
- "AI server manufacturing nearshore"
- "telecom equipment Mexico assembly"
- "automotive parts Mexico manufacturing"
- "renewable energy equipment assembly"

## Content Template for Industry Pages

### Page Structure (Recommended 8 Sections)

1. **Hero Section**
   - H1: `[Industry] Assembly for [Use Case]`
   - Subheading: Value proposition
   - Hero image (industry-specific)
   - CTA: "Request a Quote"

2. **Industry Overview**
   - Why this vertical needs specialized manufacturing
   - Key requirements (quality, speed, certifications)
   - Common challenges

3. **GPW's Capabilities for This Industry**
   - What services we offer
   - Why we're different (nearshoring, speed, quality)
   - Key differentiators

4. **Relevant Services**
   - 4-5 service cards linking to service detail pages
   - How services combine for this industry

5. **Why Mexico Manufacturing?**
   - Nearshoring benefits
   - USMCA/ITAR compliance
   - Lead time advantages
   - Cost optimization

6. **Social Proof**
   - Case study or testimonial (if available)
   - Client logos or results

7. **Standards & Certifications**
   - Relevant certifications for industry
   - Compliance badges
   - Quality assurances

8. **CTA & Contact**
   - Multiple conversion options
   - Contact information
