## Executive Summary
Research conducted on 6 major EMS competitors to document how they structure their industry-specific pages. This analysis shows page organization patterns, industry focus areas, and naming conventions used in their navigation strategies.

## Comparative Analysis

### Industry Page Organization Patterns

| Competitor | Pattern | # of Industries | Primary Structure | URL Approach |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| SEACOMP | Hub-based | 7 industries | Who We Help + Customer Size | `/who-we-help` hub |
| COASTLINE | Categorical | 2 categories | Medical vs. General Electronics | `/electronics/` grouping |
| PARPRO | Vertical-focused | 6 verticals | Equal industry treatment | `/industries/[slug]` |
| MILWAUKEE | Service-based | 0 industries | Capabilities over verticals | No industry pages |
| CREATION | Market-focused | 4 markets | Business segment based | Homepage hub |

### Key Findings

1. **Industry Breadth Ranges from 0-7**
   - PARPRO leads with 6 dedicated, distinct verticals
   - SEACOMP has 7 verticals but grouped under "Who We Help"
   - COASTLINE uses only 2 categories (Medical + Electronics)
   - Milwaukee avoids industry pages entirely

2. **URL Patterns Vary Significantly**
   - Dedicated URLs: `/industries/aerospace-defense/` (PARPRO style)
   - Hub-based: `/who-we-help/` (SEACOMP style)
   - Grouped categories: `/electronics/`, `/medical/` (COASTLINE style)
   - No dedicated URLs (Milwaukee, Creation)

3. **Segmentation Approaches**
   - **Vertical-focused:** PARPRO (6 industries, no size segment)
   - **Hybrid:** SEACOMP (7 industries + company size)
   - **Binary:** COASTLINE (Medical vs. General)
   - **Service-focused:** Milwaukee (0 industries, 5 services)
   - **Market-focused:** Creation (4 markets, not traditional industries)

4. **Industries Most Commonly Featured**
   - Medical/Life Sciences: 4/5 (SEACOMP, COASTLINE, PARPRO, Milwaukee)
   - Aerospace & Defense: 1/5 (PARPRO only)
   - Industrial/Controls: 2/5 (SEACOMP, PARPRO)
   - Communications: 1/5 (PARPRO only)
   - IoT/Smart Home: 2/5 (SEACOMP only)

5. **Page Naming Conventions**
   - Formal titles: "Medical Device Manufacturing", "Aerospace & Defense"
   - H1 consistency: Industry name is primary H1
   - Keyword focus: Industry name + manufacturing/assembly + any specialty (e.g., "ISO 13485")

## Recommended Structure for GPW

Based on this analysis, GPW should consider:

1. **Dedicated Industry Pages:** PARPRO's model is clearest for EMS (6 verticals, equal treatment)
2. **URL Structure:** `/industries/[industry-slug]/` or `/markets/[industry-slug]/`
3. **Industry Count:** Target 6-8 industries for comprehensive coverage
4. **Hub Approach:** Consider creating a `/industries/` hub page like PARPRO (vs. SEACOMP's `/who-we-help/`)
5. **Customer Segmentation:** Optional secondary segmentation (SEACOMP's SMB/Enterprise) but not required
6. **Avoid Service Shuffling:** Unlike Milwaukee, lead with industries (better SEO for vertical targeting)
