Our Company

Two Divisions, One Standard
of Excellence

Global Precision Works (GPW) is a precision manufacturing and electromechanical assembly company headquartered in Monterrey, Mexico, serving U.S. OEMs across 8 industries. The company operates two specialized divisions — Contract Manufacturing (CNC precision machining) and Electromechanical Assembly (box build, cable harness, system integration, and testing) — delivering nearshore production with same-timezone collaboration and USMCA tariff advantages.
01

Contract Manufacturing

CNC Precision Machining

GPW's Contract Manufacturing division delivers precision CNC machining for components that demand consistency at scale. From prototype runs to production volumes, we machine parts to specification across metals and engineering-grade plastics.

  • CNC Milling & Turning — multi-axis machining for complex geometries
  • Prototyping to Production — seamless transition without changing partners
  • Material Versatility — aluminum, steel, stainless, brass, and engineering plastics
  • In-process inspection and dimensional verification on every run

02

Electromechanical Assembly

From BOM to Boxed Product

Built for one thing: taking your bill of materials and delivering a fully assembled, tested, and packaged product. We handle mechanical structure, component integration, cable routing, functional testing, and everything in between.

  • Box Build Integration — mechanical + electrical + cabling + testing
  • Cable & Wire Harness Assembly — custom harnesses built to print
  • System Integration — full product assembly from chassis to firmware
  • Testing & QA — end-of-line functional testing, burn-in, and inspection
  • Enclosure & Cabinet Assembly — rack-mount, panel, and cabinet builds
