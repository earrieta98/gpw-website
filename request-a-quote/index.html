Frequently Asked Questions

RFQ & Process Questions

What can I request a quote for?

CNC machined parts, electromechanical assembly, or both in a single request. Choose a capability at the top of the form and we show only the fields that matter for your project. Not sure which you need? Select "Not sure yet" and our engineering team helps you scope it.

How quickly will I hear back?

Our engineering team reviews every submission within 24 business hours with an initial assessment. A detailed quote follows once we confirm scope, material, and tolerances. Urgent projects can be fast-tracked — tell us in the description.

Does GPW machine parts in-house?

Assembly and integration are performed in-house in Monterrey. CNC machining is produced through a managed, vetted network of Monterrey machine shops — GPW owns the engineering (DFM), quality governance, logistics, and accountability. You work with one supplier and one point of contact for either capability, with no single-shop bottleneck on capacity.

What information helps you quote accurately?

A CAD file (STEP preferred) gives exact geometry. A 2D drawing with dimensions and tolerances covers GD&T, surface finish, and threads. Add material spec, quantity, and finish where you can. Don't have all of it? Submit what you have — a sketch with dimensions or a photo of an existing part works, and we ask for what's missing.

What is a free DFM review?

Engineering Support is GPW's pre-quote service: a free Design-for-Manufacturability review on machining projects, plus material-selection and process guidance. We quote the part as designed and with our recommended changes, so you can see where to improve manufacturability or reduce cost before committing.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

No. We handle everything from prototypes and pilot builds to sustained production. Tell us your quantity and frequency and we scope pricing to match — one-time order or recurring program.

What file formats can I upload?

STEP and STP (preferred), IGES and IGS, DXF, DWG, PDF, plus ZIP or RAR for multiple files or large assemblies. We also accept BOMs and spec sheets (DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX) and images (PNG, JPG). Maximum 10 MB per upload — for larger files, email them to sales@gpw-solutions.com.

Is my project information confidential?

Yes. Every submission is treated as confidential and never shared with third parties. If you require a mutual NDA before submitting project details or files, contact us and we arrange it before you submit.
