## 🟡 SHOULD-FIX (4 items)

### Fix 3: Passive Voice — 18 Instances
**Rule:** 8.2 — Active voice throughout (minimal passive constructions)
**Acceptable for About page:** The About page template calls for "personal, credible, story-driven" tone, where some passive constructions are natural (e.g., "was founded," "is rooted"). However, 18 instances is high. The most impactful ones to fix:

1. "The facility **was designed** from the ground up" → "We designed the facility from the ground up"
2. "The facility **is designed** for flexibility" → "The facility delivers flexibility"
3. "Our team **is structured** around one principle" → "We structured our team around one principle"
4. "Assembly processes **are structured** to support" → "Assembly processes support"
5. "Facility access **is controlled** and production areas **are segregated**" → "We control facility access and segregate production areas"
6. "All project data... **are stored** in secure systems" → "We store all project data... in secure systems"

**Acceptable passive (leave as-is):** "was founded," "was engineered," "was made," "is rooted," "are trained," "is assigned" — these read naturally in an About page narrative.

**Recommendation:** Fix 6 most mechanical passive constructions; keep 12 narrative ones.
