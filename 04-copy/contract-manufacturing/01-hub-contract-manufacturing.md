## SECTION: CNC Machining Services

**Label:** From Raw Stock to Finished Part

**H2:** 5 Core Machining Services

GPW's contract manufacturing capabilities span five core processes. Most customer projects use one or two. Complex programs combine three or more — managed under a single point of contact.

**01 — CNC Milling**
3-axis, 4-axis, and 5-axis milling for parts with complex geometries, pockets, and contoured surfaces. Structural brackets, housings, manifold blocks, fixture plates — from aluminum and steel to titanium and engineering plastics. Tolerances to ±0.001".
→ See CNC Milling

**02 — CNC Turning**
Precision lathe work for cylindrical parts — shafts, pins, fittings, bushings, spacers, connectors, and threaded components. Live tooling available for parts that need both turning and milling features in a single setup.
→ See CNC Turning

**03 — Wire EDM**
Electrical discharge machining for parts that conventional cutting cannot reach. Hardened tool steel, tungsten carbide, complex internal profiles, and features with tolerances to ±0.0001". When your part demands precision in the hardest materials, EDM delivers.
→ See Wire EDM

**04 — Sheet Metal Fabrication**
Laser cutting, CNC bending, punching, and welding. Steel, stainless, aluminum, copper, and brass in gauges from 24ga to 1". Enclosures, brackets, panels, guards, chassis — cut, formed, welded, and finished.
→ See Sheet Metal

**05 — Surface Finishing**
Anodizing (Type II and III), powder coating, plating (nickel, zinc, chrome), passivation, heat treatment, bead blasting, electropolishing, and laser marking. We deliver complete parts — machined, finished, and ready to install — not raw stock that needs another vendor.
→ See Surface Finishing
